Our Story

The heart of Africa
in every aisle

Afro Fresh Store was born from a passion to bring the authentic flavours of home to Nottingham's vibrant community. We source directly from farms and markets across the continent — because quality is non-negotiable.

Every product carries a story: of harvests, of recipes handed down through generations, of culture kept alive across thousands of miles.
